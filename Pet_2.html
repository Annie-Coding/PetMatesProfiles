About
Prince and Lady's background

Ned is an 11 year old mastiff x staffy who is the best dog in the world. He is a people person and is happiest in the company of those who love him and provide never-ending pats.

Education
What Ned has learned

Ned has been obedience trained as well as being house trained. he currently boasts a 100% positive track record of no accidents. Nice going Ned!

History
Where Ned has lived

Unfortunetly, Ned missed out on a lot of deserved love in his early life, spending over 4 months living at the RSPCA. He arrived at his forever home 7 years ago. During this time, he has become very familiar with the back streets and local parks of Brunswick, and more recently Northcote. His favourite suburb is Northcote: there are lots of kids around for extra pats :)

Skills and proficencies
Why Ned is a special little snowflakes:

Ned happens to be a proficient tail wagger, as well as reliably sitting when asked (especially for treats). He is quite capable of jumping in and out of the car as needed to go on car adventures, as well as barking when the doorbell rings to ensure protection of the house. As all good boys are, he is adept at selecting just the perfect toy to support greeting his person at the front door on arrival home from work. He is also well known for his amazing architecture skills, building impressive pillow cubbies on the bed.

Awards
Where Ned's shining personality shines even harder

Ned takes the competition away on Best All Round Good Boy (large breed category) and Best Dog in the World (all categories) in 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019 and still going strong.

Interests
What Ned does for fun

Ned enjoys walks, treats, pats, snoozes and a healthy dose of cuddles everyday.
